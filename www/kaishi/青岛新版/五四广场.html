54 square named after "54 movements", is one of the signs of Qingdao now. Compared with the music square, there are more tourists here. The median square spiral shaped like a flourishing wheal sculpture called "May wind", highlighting the solemn, solid, vigorous patriotism. With the "wind of May" as the center, extending westward, there is a romantic European style music square, stretching eastward to the Olympic sailing stadium. Similar to the sea scenery, different landscape style, compared to Huiquan Bay and Zhanqiao scenic coastline, the scenery here is more fashionable, more modern Qingdao. Around the square is the city's highest land price of CBD, leaned on the beach railing, looking back on this modern economy created by the modern landscape, you will feel the unique Qingdao heroic and warmth.
